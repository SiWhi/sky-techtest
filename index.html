Best of British Drama
Downton Abbey Series 1-4
Multi-award winning period drama that traces the lives of the aristocratic Crawley family through the first decades of the 20th century. Maggie Smith stars in this epic tale of scandal, tragedy and love.
Scott and Bailey Series 1-3
Sparks fly as two very different characters – detectives DC Rachel Bailey and DC Janet Scott – are asked to work as a partnership within an elite Manchester police unit that specialises in solving murder cases.
Best of British Comedy
Stella Series 1-3
Laugh yourself daft with this gentle and touching comedy – created by and starring Ruth Jones – about single mother Stella and her family and friends set in the heart of the Welsh valleys.
The IT Crowd Series 1-4
This riotous sitcom set in an IT department won a hatful of Emmys and launched some stellar careers. Written by Graham Linehan and starring Chris O'Dowd, Richard Ayoade and Katherine Parkinson.
Last Chance
Sherlock Series 1-2
Benedict Cumberbatch and Martin Freeman make a perfect 21st century Holmes and Watson in this hugely successful modern-day adaptation of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle’s classic detective stories.
